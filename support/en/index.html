The game

How do I play Arrow Path GO?

Tap an arrow to send it off the board. That only works if the lane in front of it is completely clear all the way out of the grid. If another arrow is in the way, the tap costs you a life. Clear the board to complete the level.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. All levels are in the app and you can play fully offline. Only the online leaderboard needs a connection: if you play offline, the app keeps your result and sends it once you are back online.

How do hints work?

A hint points out an arrow you can safely clear right now. You have a limited number of hints; when they run out you can earn an extra one by watching a rewarded video.

What happens when time runs out?

On levels with a time limit you get one chance per level to earn extra time by watching a rewarded video. The clock then only starts again once you tap an arrow — just like at the start of a level.

Lives and the daily streak

How do I get new lives?

In three ways:

  • Daily check-in: open the app on a new day and your lives are topped up to 3. If you already had 3, you get a bonus life on top.
  • Moving to the next level: you get one back, up to a maximum of 3.
  • Rewarded video: if you've lost all your lives, a video refills your pool completely.
What does the “+2” next to my lives mean?

Those are your bonus lives: extra lives saved on top of the standard 3 by checking in every day. By default you can save up to 5 (so 8 lives in total). Reaching a streak milestone raises that maximum.

How does the daily streak work?

Your streak counts the number of consecutive days you have opened the app. Miss a day and the counter starts over — but you keep the bonus lives you already saved.

I missed a day. Is my streak gone?

Not straight away: you get one free streak freeze per month. If you miss exactly one day and haven't used your freeze that month, your streak is protected automatically and simply continues.

What do milestones give me?

At 7, 30 and 100 consecutive days you earn a badge and the maximum number of bonus lives you can save goes up permanently.

Leaderboard and privacy

How do I change my leaderboard name?

Go to Settings and change your display name (3 to 20 characters: letters, digits, spaces, hyphens and underscores). That name is visible to other players, so it's best not to use your real name.

What data do you collect?

Your progress, lives, streak and settings stay on your device. For the online leaderboard we use an anonymous account (a random ID, with no email address or password) and store your display name and result for each completed level. Full details are in the privacy policy.

How do I delete my data?

Local data (progress, lives, settings) is erased by uninstalling the app. To have your leaderboard results removed as well, email smartapps.ev@gmail.com and include your display name.

My score isn't in the leaderboard

Check your internet connection: without one, your result is stored and sent automatically later. Only your personal best per level appears in the leaderboard, so a score lower than your own best does not replace it.

Settings and ads

How do I change the theme?

Go to Settings → Theme and pick light, dark or “system”. New installs start in the light theme.

Which languages does the app support?

The app interface is available in Dutch, English and German. You pick the language in Settings.

Can I turn off sound, music or vibration?

Yes, Settings has separate switches for sound effects, background music and vibration (haptics).

Can I turn off the ads?

Ads keep the app free and cannot be switched off. Rewarded videos are always optional: you choose whether to watch one for a hint, an extra life or extra time.
